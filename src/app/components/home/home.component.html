How It Works

1. Grind & dose (15–25s)

Add beans to the hopper and select your grind time. The integrated grinder doses consistently for your basket size.
2. Tamp & lock in (10s)

Distribute, tamp firmly, and lock the 58mm portafilter into the group head.
3. Pre‑infuse & extract (25–30s)

Choose single or double. TerraBrew pre‑infuses, then extracts at 9 bar for a syrupy, even shot.
4. Steam & serve (30–45s)

Texture milk with the powerful steam wand and pour. Auto‑clean cycle runs when you're done.

FAQ

Does it work with light and dark roasts?

Yes. Adjust grind and dose; the PID keeps temperature stable for both profiles.

 Is it compatible with 220–240V power?

We offer region‑specific models. Choose your voltage at checkout.

 How often do I descale?

The machine tracks water hardness and usage, then reminds you—typically every 2–3 months.

 What's the return policy?

Try it for 30 days. If it's not a fit, return it in original condition for a full refund.

 Can I use pre‑ground coffee?

Yes. Use the included single‑wall baskets and set the grinder to bypass.
